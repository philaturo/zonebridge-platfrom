# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

ZoneBridge is an open-source project built for the Zone01 Kisumu community.

We are committed to fostering an open, respectful, and inclusive environment where apprentices, alumni, mentors, and contributors can collaborate professionally and learn from one another.

Participation in this community means treating others with respect, communicating constructively, and contributing toward a positive engineering culture.

---

## Expected Behavior

Members of the community are expected to:

- Be respectful and considerate in all interactions.
- Welcome constructive feedback and differing perspectives.
- Focus discussions on technical ideas rather than individuals.
- Share knowledge openly and help others learn.
- Assume positive intent while communicating professionally.
- Respect differing levels of experience and encourage participation.
- Contribute in ways that strengthen the community.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or intimidation.
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Offensive or abusive comments.
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional community.

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct.

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions, comments, discussions, or other interactions that violate these guidelines.

Repeated or serious violations may result in temporary or permanent removal from the project community.

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it privately to the project maintainers.

Reports will be handled respectfully, confidentially, and in good faith.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including:

- GitHub Issues
- Pull Requests
- Discussions
- Documentation
- Community communication channels
- Any events officially organized under the ZoneBridge project
